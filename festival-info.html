FESTIVAL INFORMATION

The sections below aim to give you all the information you need about Whelnett Wedfest.

Location

Poplar Farm, Clevedon, Somerset BS21 6UN Map

Travel

  • Car Poplar Farm is just 10 minutes from Junction 20 M5. Please be weary of cyclists and pedestrians along the country roads near the farm.
  • Parking There is plenty of parking available at Poplar Farm but it is not immediately next to camping and bell tent areas. Follow the signage when you arrive.
  • Train The closest train station is Yatton. Either pre-book a taxi or ask a friend to collect you – it is about a 10 minute drive.
  • Cycle If you are feeling adventurous, Poplar Farm sits along National Cycle Route 33. North Somerset has beautiful countryside to explore along the way.
  • Taxis Book a taxi or Uber in advance as they are not frequent in the area. Local companies: North Somerset Taxis, Streets Ahead Taxis, Triangle Cars Clevedon.

Accommodation

  • Nearby stays If camping or glamping isn’t your style, Poplar Farm is close to Clevedon and Yatton for hotels and Airbnbs.
  • Festival vibes We encourage you to bring the festival spirit and rent a bell tent if you can.
  • Glamping guide See our glamping options page for details on what to expect and how to make the most of your stay.

Dress Code

We encourage earthy tones and bright colours for the handfasting ceremony as it is thought to bring positive energy to the occasion. We suggest more relaxed suits and flowy, comfortable dresses. Shoes with thin heels, such as stilettos, are not advised as we will be on grass for much of the day.

Bring vibrant, festival themed clothing for the rest of the day and be prepared for whatever the weather may bring!
